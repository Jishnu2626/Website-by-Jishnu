About Us

Founded in 2020, Biro's Sandwich Co. is a culinary gem in Satya Niketan, New Delhi, dedicated to crafting artisanal sandwiches that elevate everyday dining. Our journey began with a simple vision: to combine fresh, high-quality ingredients with bold flavors, creating sandwiches that are both comforting and extraordinary. From our signature Paneer Tikka to the indulgent Club Sandwich, every item is made with passion and precision.

We source our produce from local farms and bakeries, ensuring every bite supports our community and delivers unmatched freshness. As a family-run business, we take pride in being a gathering place for students, professionals, and food lovers in Satya Niketan. Join us to experience the art of sandwiches, where quality meets creativity.
